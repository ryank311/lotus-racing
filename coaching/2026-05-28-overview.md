### Three confirmed effects

1. **Mist removes ~−4-6 s/lap** (sessions 1-2 vs session 4 same day): ~17% lat-G reduction. Track was effectively wet/damp.

2. **Cold ambient (<13°C) on cold tires removes ~−7 s/lap** (session 5 vs session 6 same morning): 30e5bb52 best 2:21.33 vs ebe23e23 best 2:15.83. Both at 12.2°C ambient. Difference: ebe23e23 had warm tires from the previous-day cycle. Peak lat-G: 12.51 cold → 13.52 once warm.

3. **Peak lat-G correlates with peak laptime**: sessions with lat-G < 13 m/s² (≈1.32g) all run 2:20+. Sessions with lat-G > 14 m/s² (≈1.43g) all run 2:14-2:17. **The car has a clear grip threshold below which lap times collapse non-linearly.**

### Day-by-day improvement
- **Friday** (4 sessions): 2:20.18 → 2:16.42 = **−3.76 s** as track dried
- **Saturday** (3 sessions): 2:21.33 → 2:14.11 = **−7.22 s** as tires heat-cycled in
- **Sunday** (4 sessions): 2:13.97 → ... → 2:14.50 = mid-day regression from traffic (983fe6a8) and heat, then recovered

The single biggest improvement-driver across the weekend is **tire temperature in the working window** — every session that ran below ~13 m/s² peak lat-G lost 4-6+ s/lap.

## 6. Prioritised recommendations

### #1 — Late-brake T12 Oak Tree. **Expected gain: ~0.25-0.30 s in S7**
- **Evidence:** 3169a432 L5 demonstrated T13 entry 29.05 m/s vs PB-lap 25.41 — same apex, same exit, **+3.64 m/s of carry into the brake zone.**
- **Action:** push brake initiation from ~3950 m to ~4000 m. Trace at PB-lap dist 3950 m shows accel_x = **−10.98 m/s² (-1.12g)**; the car has decel headroom (peak braking elsewhere in the dataset reaches −12.5 m/s² = −1.27g). The limit is commitment, not pad/tire capacity.
- **Risk:** medium. Locking up here blocks the corner exit. If the rear becomes light, reduce brake bias rearward 1 click.

### #2 — Carry T4 → T5 momentum. **Expected gain: ~0.35-0.45 s in S2+S3**
- **Evidence:** 0d9decc9 L7 (the spin lap) hit T4 apex 28.67 + T5 apex 21.63 *before* the later T8/T9 spin. PB lap: 26.88 / 20.14.
- **Action:** later turn-in at T4, more trail-brake to rotate, accept track-out further right. The Exige's stiff front (550 lb/in) punishes early turn-in.
- **Risk:** low — T4-T5 is a forgiving error zone.

### #3 — Commit to T8 Climbing Esses 2 (post-rake config). **Expected gain: ~0.15-0.20 s in S4**
- **Evidence:** 5 clean laps demonstrate T8 apex ≥ 38.5 m/s; PB lap is the only clean lap stuck at 37.66. Lat-G in T8 zone reaches **11.84 m/s² (1.21g)** on the fastest laps. The PB lap is using only ~95% of the available cornering grip here.
- **Action:** treat T8 as flat-throttle. The April rake + damper changes have demonstrably planted the rear (peak lat-G in the dataset is now 14-15 m/s² consistently); the lift habit is a holdover from pre-setup days.
- **Risk:** high-consequence corner (blind crest). Validate in one deliberate flat-T8 attempt on a hot-tire lap with clear track before committing to a normal hot lap.
