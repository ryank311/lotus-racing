## 6. Prioritised recommendations

### #1 — Late-brake T12 Oak Tree. **Expected gain: 0.25-0.30 s in S7**
- **Evidence:** 3169a432 L5 demonstrated T13 entry 29.05 m/s vs PB-lap 25.41 (+3.64 m/s). Same apex, same exit. Demonstrated once in 116 laps — repeatable but unrepeated.
- **Action:** push brake initiation from ~3950 m to ~4000 m. Confirmed grip headroom: peak braking elsewhere on the lap reaches −12.5 m/s² (-1.27g) vs PB-lap T12 zone at −10.9 m/s² (-1.11g). The pad/tire system has ~15% more decel capacity than is being used.
- **Risk:** medium. Lock-up here ruins the corner exit onto a non-back-straight.

### #2 — Carry T4 → T5 momentum. **Expected gain: 0.35-0.45 s in S2+S3**
- **Evidence:** 0d9decc9 L7 demonstrated T4 apex 28.67 + T5 apex 21.63 cleanly *before* the later spin in T8/T9. Pattern consistent across the April sessions too (1eb146a6 L1 hit T4 apex 24.73 with the wider, faster line — although this run had its own issues elsewhere).
- **Action:** later turn-in at T4, more trail-brake to rotate, accept track-out further right. The Exige's 550 lb/in front spring punishes early turn-in.
- **Risk:** low — T4-T5 is a forgiving error zone.

### #3 — Commit to T8 Climbing Esses 2 (flat-throttle goal). **Expected gain: 0.15-0.20 s in S4**
- **Evidence:** 5 clean laps demonstrate T8 apex ≥38.5 m/s. PB lap sits at 37.66. **T8 lat-G on the fastest laps reaches 11.8 m/s² (1.20 g) — well below the car's demonstrated 14+ m/s² limit** in tight corners. The blind crest is being driven conservatively.
- **Action:** treat T8 as flat-throttle on a hot-tire lap. The April → May progression confirms the platform supports this; only commitment is missing.
- **Risk:** high-consequence corner (blind crest at speed). Validate with a single deliberate flat-T8 attempt before normal hot lap.
